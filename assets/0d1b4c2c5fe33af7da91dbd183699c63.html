ABOUT US

Henry has blended his past experience, to develop a key formula with his

successful building inspections.


He completed his Carpentry apprenticeship with AV Jennings on new home buildings. From there he moved into multi story construction and formwork where he worked on various buildings in Adelaide such as the Hyatt, Hilton and Convention Centre. He opened his own business called Inside Out Maintenance and Carpentry and operated successfully under his own builder’s license for many years. Henry was offered the role of Assessor and Construction Supervisor for a company called Rolfe and Bailey quoting, working on and overseeing the reconstruction of properties damaged by fire, motor vehicle accidents, weather incidents, termite infestation and various other accidental damage claims. The knowledge Henry has amassed over many years in the construction and insurance industries provide him with a huge advantage over his competitors and places Inside Out Home Inspections ahead of the rest if you are purchasing a home or investment property.
